Have any questions?
  • Do you offer Managed WordPress hosting?
    The term "Managed WordPress" has been so overused, like "cloud hosting", that the true definition has been lost in translation. So instead, we chose to name our service Smarter WordPress hosting as we believe it is an accurate description of what we provide.

    It is, in our opinion, the best WordPress hosting platform in Australia, with access to dozens of self-managed features such as cloning and staging, a security advisor, premium SEO tools, enterprise backups, smart updates with visual and AI indicators, and much more.
  • Can I host my email with Zuver?
    All of our hosting services come with basic email hosting included. However, if email is important to your business we strongly recommend speaking to our sales team about purchasing a dedicated email solution.

    Not only will your email still continue to operate if your website is down, but it will allow for greater mailbox sizes and more features.
  • Is Zuver reliable?
    Zuver delivers a reliable hosting service and cheap domain names every day. Most of our servers have an uptime rating of 100% thanks to the premium DELL cloud hardware they are hosted from.
