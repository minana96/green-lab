The Karnali Project

WorldLink has always remained committed to deliver excellence in service and development. Hence, our vision is to connect everyone, anywhere. This took us to 8 rural districts of Karnali region, where we are able to provide internet services for the first time. This mammoth impossible task to connect Karnali to the world would not have been possible without the active support from NTA, Local Communities, Government Officials & untiring determination and hard work of the WorldLink team.
